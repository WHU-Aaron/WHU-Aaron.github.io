### snowwwww

1.使用kali自带的工具分离图片

2.傅里叶变化解密图片水印

```matlab
%% extract watermark
FA2=fft2(FAO);
G=(FA2-FA)/alpha;
GG=G;
for i=1:imsize(1)*0.5
    for j=1:imsize(2)
        GG(M(i),N(j),:)=G(i,j,:);
    end
end
for i=1:imsize(1)*0.5
    for j=1:imsize(2)
        GG(imsize(1)+1-i,imsize(2)+1-j,:)=GG(i,j,:);
    end
end
figure,imshow(GG);title('extracted watermark');
%imwrite(uint8(GG),'extracted watermark.jpg');
```
